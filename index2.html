About Me

Friends are the most valuable things in the world. In this world everything can be happy. This is a happy place, little squirrels live here and play. If you do too much it's going to lose its effectiveness. We tell people sometimes: we're like drug dealers, come into town and get everybody absolutely addicted to painting, it doesn't take much to get you addicted. Nice little clouds playing around in the sky. Maybe there's a happy little waterfall happening over here. All you need to paint is a few tools, a little instruction, and a vision in your mind.
